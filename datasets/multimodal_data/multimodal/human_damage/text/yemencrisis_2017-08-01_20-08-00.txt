Rifah stands outside her tent with her son and two daughters in a Internally Displaced Person (IDP) camp in Yemen. We have been working in the camp since late 2015, providing humanitarian assistance with safe drinking water and other life saving humanitarian aid. 
Millions in the country are one step away from famine. Yemen is also now suffering the largest ever outbreak of cholera since records began.
