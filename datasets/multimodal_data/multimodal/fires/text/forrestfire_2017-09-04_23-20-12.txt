09.03.2017 | California Governor Jerry Brown has declared a state of emergency for Los Angeles county due to the ongoing La Tuna Canyon wildfire burning near Burbank, California. The fire is the largest of its kind in Los Angeles city history. More than 1,000 firefighters from across the state and federal levels are battling the blaze which has already destroyed 3 homes and has scorched over 7,000 acres.
