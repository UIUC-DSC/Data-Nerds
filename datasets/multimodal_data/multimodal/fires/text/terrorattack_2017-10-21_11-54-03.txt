#Somalia death toll rises to 358
:

The death toll in last week's #TerrorAttack in Somalia has risen to 358.

Hundreds more are wounded and at least 56 people remain unaccounted for.

A truck filled with explosives was detonated on a busy street in the capital #Mogadishu last weekend.
