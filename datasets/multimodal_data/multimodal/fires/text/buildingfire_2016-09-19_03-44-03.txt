On September 15, 2016 Firefighters were dispatched to a reported structure fire on the 500 block of 105th Ave. E27 arrived and reported fire showing from the rear of a 2 story residential. E27, E20 and E26 stretched multiple hose lines to the front and rear of the structure for interior operations and exposures. E29 arrived and assumed RIC duties. On arrival Truck 7 laddered the structure and cut multiple ventilation holes.
Oakland Firefighters are proud to serve and protect the citizens and visitors of the City of Oakland. #oakland #fire #workingfire #buildingfire
