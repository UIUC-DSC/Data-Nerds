Clear Lake Presbyterian Church in Houston has been a supporter of Fonds Verettes in Haiti, and was on the front lines of responding to the 2010 earthquake and Hurricane Matthieu. Then over the summer, natural disaster struck at home as Hurricane Harvey flooded the city.
.
The lessons from helping in Haiti allowed Clear Lake to show up fully engaged in Houston.
.
It was a great reminder of the importance of approaching people as equals, said Pastor Katrina Pennington. "No one wants to be on the receiving end all the time. The lessons we learned in Haiti were put employed as we worked in Houston, with upwardly mobile people as they recovered."
