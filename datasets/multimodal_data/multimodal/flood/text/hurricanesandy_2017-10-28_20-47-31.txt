While the flooding (pictured here) during Sandy was devastating, residents of Sea Bright, New Jersey are no strangers to what is known as “nuisance” or “sunny day” flooding. Scientists have documented a sharp increase in this type of flooding (which occurs during high tides) in recent years, a phenomenon that has been escalated as sea levels continue to rise.
