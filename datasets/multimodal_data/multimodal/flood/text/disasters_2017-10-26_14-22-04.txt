At least 26 people died and several more were badly injured in an explosion at a fireworks warehouse on the outskirts of Jakarta on Thursday morning (26/10), BeritaSatu TV reported. The warehouse, which belongs to Panca Buana Cahaya Sukses, is in Kosambi, a subdistrict of Tangerang, Banten. (Photo courtesy of Twitter/@naidusudhakar)
