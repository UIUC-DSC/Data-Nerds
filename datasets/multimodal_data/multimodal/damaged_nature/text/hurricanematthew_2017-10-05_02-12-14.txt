One year ago today, #HurricaneMatthew made landfall on northern Haiti, bringing rain and destruction.
.
Today we give thanks for the resilient spirit of our Haitian brothers and sisters - and your gifts that help them recover.
.
Pictured: Asney Dolan fits a board he used to repair his fishing boat, “Secours d’en Haut” (Help From Above). His village, Jean Macoute, was ravaged by the storm and his fishing boat was destroyed, as were so many others in his community.
.
But with your help, he and others are rebuilding. Praise be to God!
.
