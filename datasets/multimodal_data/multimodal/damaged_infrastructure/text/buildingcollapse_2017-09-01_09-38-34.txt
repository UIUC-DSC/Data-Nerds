20 din ke bacche ke saath 34 logo ki hui maut, #BhendiBazar #BuildingCollapse

Raat bhar chale bachav karya me karib 45 logo ko rescue kiya gaya aur #JJ #Hospital me admit kiya gaya.

Ab tak 34 logo ki maut hui hai, jisme 23 male aur 10 female hai.

Kal Subah karib 8.30 am, Bhendibazar ke Pakhmodia Street me Hussainiwala Buidling collapsed hui thi.

125 saal purani ye building G+ 3 thi lekin 25 saal pahele aur 3 floor extra banaye gaye the. 
Building ke Ground floor par hotel me kam karne wale log rahete the jo khana bana rahe the, 2nd, 3rd floor par 5 family raheti thi, Play Group School bhi is buidling me tha. Lekin ye school ka time 10.00 am hone se wo is hadse se bach gaye.

#CM #Fadnavis ke saath, BJP MLA Ashish Shelar, Congress MLA Amin Patel, Housing Minister Prakash Shah ne site par visit ki. Aur mrutak mi family ko Rs. 10 lakh dene ki ghoshna bhi ki.

Rs. 5 lakh CM ki taraf se jabki Rs. 5 lakh Relief and Rahabilitation Minister Chandrakant Patil ki taraf se diye jayenge.

CM Fadnavis ne is hadse ke bare me enquiry ke adhesh diye hai
