July 24, 2014

A building in downtown Kingman has partially collapsed.

The building, located at the intersection of Main and Sherman, collapsed around 2:40 p.m. Thursday.

Police say no one was in the building when the collapse happened, and no one was hurt.

Emergency crews are working with other building owners in the area. No surroundings buildings were damaged.

The block has been closed due to safety concerns.

The cause is under investigation. Check Kake.com or our fb page for updates.
